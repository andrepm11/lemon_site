  • What’s in a Verb Bar? Verb bars are made with almond butter, rolled oats, puffed brown rice, organic unsweetened cocoa powder, vanilla, and caffeine from organic green tea, then lightly sweetened with organic agave nectar and a hint of brown sugar. The bars are made in a certified gluten-free facility.

  • Why do you use caffeine from green tea in Verb Bars? We’ve chosen organic green tea because it’s healthy, clean, and provides a long-lasting energy boost. Caffeine from tea is absorbed over a longer period of time than that from other sources. That’s why coffee drinkers usually report feeling more focused and energized after a Verb Bar than after coffee.
